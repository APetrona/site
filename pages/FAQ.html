FAQs -Frequently Asked Questions

What types of crimes can I report?
You can report various types of crimes such as robbery, assault, burglary, fraud, and more. Our system is designed to handle a wide range of incidents.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we take the security of your information seriously. Rest assured that the details you provide are secure and will only be accessed by relevant personnel for investigative purposes.
How do I report a crime?
To report a crime, navigate to the "Report Crime" page on our website. Fill out the required information, including details about the incident, and submit the form.
What Information Should I Include in My Report?
Include crucial details such as the type of crime, date, time, and a comprehensive description. Providing accurate and specific information helps law enforcement in their investigation.
Is my report anonymous?
Yes, our system provides you with a pseudonym or an alias in order to hide your identity. you have the option to submit an anonymous report. We respect your privacy, and anonymous submissions are also valuable for crime prevention.
What Happens After I Submit a Report?
After submitting a report, it undergoes review by our team. You may receive a confirmation, and if further information is needed, we may contact you discreetly.
How Can I Track the Status of My Report?
A tracking feature is available for registered users. Log in to your account to check the status of your report and receive updates on its progress.
Is the System Accessible 24/7?
Yes, our crime reporting system is accessible 24/7. You can submit reports at any time, and our team will review them promptly.
Who Has Access to My Report?
Only authorized personnel within law enforcement or relevant authorities have access to the information provided in your report. Your privacy is a top priority.
What Should I Do in Case of Emergency?
In case of an emergency or immediate danger, please contact local emergency services. Our online reporting system is designed for non-emergency situations.
