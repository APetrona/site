Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [2023]

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing LindaJiJi, the crime reporting system dedicated to promoting safety in Kenya. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2.Information we collect

User Information: When you register or submit a report, we collect personal information such as your name, ID number, and contact details.
Report Information: Details provided in crime reports, including crime type, description, and relevant details.
Anonymous Reports: Users have the option to submit reports anonymously.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:
Process and respond to crime reports.
Improve and personalize user experience.
Communicate important updates and notifications.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 99.9% secure.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Information may be shared with law enforcement or relevant authorities for investigative purposes.

6. Compliance with Laws

We adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Whistleblowers Act of 2021 in Kenya. Your rights under these regulations are respected and upheld.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted.
If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us at DCI Kenya
